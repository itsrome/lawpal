✨ Features
Everything LawPal can do for you.

3 Languages

Works in English, Amharic (አማርኛ), and Afaan Oromo — the three most spoken languages in Ethiopia.

Ethiopian Law

Answers are grounded in real Ethiopian laws: Constitution, Civil Code, Labour Proclamation, Traffic Proclamation, and more.

Text Simplification

Replaces 100+ complex legal terms with plain language. Breaks long sentences into shorter, readable ones.

Instant Answers

Rule-based AI gives you answers in under 2 seconds. No internet required after setup.

Know Your Rights

Covers tenant rights, employment, traffic, student rights, family law, criminal law, and constitutional rights.

For Everyone

Designed for students, renters, drivers, employees — anyone who needs to understand legal information without a lawyer.

ℹ️ About LawPal
A student project from Madda Walabu University.

LawPal is an AI-based system designed to make legal information more understandable and accessible. It uses rule-based logic and basic NLP to simplify legal text and answer common legal questions.
